This is for you

There was a moment I keep coming back to.

A hillside.
A clear sky.

30.11.25

I gave you my first flower —

and you took it.

No big words.
Just the sun slowly deciding to leave.

So today, I give you this flower too.

I didn’t know it then,
but that was me choosing you.

(Some moments stay warm.)
